Features

The application contains an impressive set of features that makes it easy to eliminate noise and focus on signals.

The Most Advanced Execution Screener

Looking for options activity pointing to the next big buyout, or for a delta-neutral strategy that exploits elevated volatility? Whatever your strategy, Quotail can help you find them with our execution scanner. Choose from over 40 criteria while creating the most expressive scans imaginable.

Next, test your scan's historical performance against our massive database of all trades in the options market to see how many results your scan yields, and most importantly, how profitable they ended up being.

Real-time Alerts

Now that you've built a scan to match your strategy, it's time to activate it on live data. Your scan will be run against every options trade as it crosses the wire, and you'll receive alerts on those matching trades.

We've also created a scrolling activity feed to present your alerts in an easy to digest format. At a quick glance, you can see what the most relevant trades are and add them to your watchlist.

In-Depth Analysis

Dig into the order flow for a specific ticker with our analysis tools. You can graph option contracts across various metrics, and jump to different day activity by clicking on a region of the graph.

Options Volume Heatmap

Not sure where to begin hunting? Start with a bird's eye view of the options activity for today in our volume heatmap. Tickers with an unusual spike in options volume show up as larger squares, and those with more bullish aggregate flow are greener.
