How It Works

The Quotail Process enables users to quickly discover trade strategies and see their potential return.
Our process is built on saving users time and maximizing profitability.

1. Create a Strategy

Create custom scans on options trade data using over 40 different parameters to match your trading strategy.

2. Backtest Your Strategy

Apply your scans on our historical dataset to see how profitable your strategy has been for traders in the past.

3. Get Real Time Alerts

Put your strategy to the test on live market data. Get notified as trades matching your criteria are executed.

Features

The application contains an impressive set of features that makes it easy to eliminate noise and focus on signals.

Scan Creation

What's your approach to the market? Looking for options activity pointing to the next big buyout, or for a delta-neutral strategy that exploits elevated volatility? Whatever opportunities you seek, Quotail can help you find them.

You can get started by using our preset scans or create your own. Express your strategy using over 40 different criteria on both the underlying as well as the option. Quantity, volatility, underlying stock price, open interest, industry/sector to name a few. No programming necessary.

Backtesting

Now that you got a strategy in hand, put it to the test with our backtesting solution. Built on historical options time and sales data, our backtesting engine applies your filters against past trades in the market and measures profitability over their lifetime.

It's the first and only backtesting engine focused on actual trades, not hypothetical market conditions, so you can see how large traders that followed your strategy fared.

Our research has shown that certain types of big bets consistently perform well. This is our way of separating the smart money from the dumb money.

Real Time Alerts

Once you feel confident with the results, it's time to set it out in the wild. Your strategy will be screened on all trades that come across the wire, and when a trade filters through, you get an instant notification. Stay in tune with all the relevant action, and block the noise.

Strategy Leaderboard

Curious how your strategy stacks up against others? See the performance of other stratgies and follow proven winners in the Quotail community.
