Meet the

Founding Team

Our Story

Four friends, who met while studying Engineering at University of Maryland, reunited to solve the problem of wealth management and investing. As trading enthusiasts, we saw a need for a better way to generate and analyze investment opportunities, and thus, Quotail.

Sahil Shah Co-Founder / CEO

Sahil is an experienced software engineer and a top notch trader. He has his hands in every part of the business, from engineering and design to marketing and sales. When he's not trading iron condors or pitching Quotail, you can find Sahil biking around New York City trying his best to avoid cabs.

Prateek Kukreja Co-Founder / Product & Design

While being formerly trained in Electrical Engineering, Prateek started his career in software engineering and then transitioned into product management. At Quotail, he does everything related to design and user experience, while also running marketing and sales for the company. When he's not shipping products, Prateek enjoys playing tennis, mixing music, and gaming on his Xbox.

Amir Majedi Co-Founder / Engineering

Amir is a masterful Software Engineer with a creative eye for front end development. If he's not automating, scripting, or designing, he's building out beautiful user interfaces. With a background in Math and Computer Science, Amir brings to the table domain knowledge with the ability to execute. On the weekends, you can find Amir on the autocross track or playing Gran Turismo.

Janahan Sivaraman Co-Founder / Engineering & Chief Fitness Officer

Janahan is an infrastruture guru and a fitness enthusiast. He manages everything data at Quotail and ensures Quotail servers never go down. When he's not putting out fires, he's cooking up amazing dishes for the team, deadlifting, and getting rest of the team in shape.
